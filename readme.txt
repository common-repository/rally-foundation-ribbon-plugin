Help support the Rally Foundation aand childrens cancer wareness by putting a ribbon on your WordPress site or network.


== Description ==


A small ribbon with the Rally Foundation logo and NO Kids CANCER will be put on in the top right corner of your website, linking to the Rally Foundation (http://www.rallyfoundation.org) website.
== Installation ==


1. Download the plugin zip archive.

2. Extract it in your `wp-content/plugins` folder or upload via admin panel.

3. Browse to your plugins section and active the plugin.
